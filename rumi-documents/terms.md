# Rumi: Terms of Service & Legal Framework

This document governs the tokenization, acquisition, and trade of Peruvian stones on the Rumi platform. By interacting with Rumi NFTs on the Hedera network, you agree to these terms. These terms do not replace official government regulations.

## 1. Legal Basis for Mineral Commercialization
Rumi operates under the legal framework of the Republic of Peru. All tokenized assets are protected by the following statutes:
- **Free Trade (Article 4):** Pursuant to the Ley General de Minería (DS Nº 014-92-EM), the internal and external trade of mineral products is free (**Libre Comercialización**). Buyers must ensure stones are sourced through legal channels.
- **Legal Possession (Article 6 & 263):** The possession of minerals by non-concession holders (artisans, retailers, and collectors) is legally recognized. The retail invoice or point of discovery serves as the established legal start of provenance.

## 2. Certified Origin
Assets backed by REINFO or VUCE documentation.

## 3. Cultural Heritage (Patrimonio Cultural)
Rumi strictly prohibits the tokenization of items classified as National Cultural Heritage.
- In compliance with [Ministerio de Cultura](https://www.gob.pe/cultura) regulations, no stone identified as a pre-Columbian artifact or protected historical object may be minted.
- The Validation Layer (multi-sig) provides a preliminary check but does not supersede official government determination.
- Users are responsible for verifying heritage status before minting or export.

## 4. Export Compliance & Taxes
- **Export Responsibility:** Rumi metadata assists with [SUNAT](https://www.sunat.gob.pe) and [VUCE](https://www.vuce.gob.pe) filings, but the physical exporter is solely responsible for the accuracy of customs declarations. NFTs are a supporting digital "Packing List" and "Certificate of Origin," not a substitute for final clearance.
- **Taxation:** International exports of stones are generally exempt from IGV (General Sales Tax) under Peruvian export law, provided all SUNAT protocols are met.

## 5. Metadata and Physical Pairing
- **Digital-Physical Bond:** NFT metadata (HIP-412 compliant) includes optical fingerprints (inclusions) stored on IPFS.
- **Verification:** Any tampering with the physical stone or its associated "Rumi ID Card" nullifies the digital provenance record. Mismatches invalidate provenance.

## 6. Disclaimer of Liability
Rumi is a platform for the tokenization of Real World Assets (RWAs).
- Values like market price, geological rarity, and artisanal craftsmanship are subject to fluctuation.
- Rumi does not provide financial or investment advice. Tokens represent ownership of a specific physical specimen and its associated cultural data.
- Rumi NFTs are collectibles tied to physical specimens; they are not securities, derivatives, or regulated financial instruments.
