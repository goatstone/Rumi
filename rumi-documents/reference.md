# Rumi: Regulatory and Technical Reference Directory (2026 Updated)

This directory contains the essential links and statutory references for the Rumi project to maintain legal compliance with Peruvian law and technical alignment with the Hedera ecosystem.

## 1. Peruvian Regulatory & Export Authorities
*   **[SUNAT](https://www.sunat.gob.pe):** (Superintendencia Nacional de Aduanas y de Administración Tributaria). The authority for tax and customs.
    *   *Reference:* [Customs Tariff Search (Arancel de Aduanas)](http://www.aduanet.gob.pe)
*   **[PROMPERÚ](https://www.gob.pe):** The Commission for the Promotion of Peru for Export and Tourism.
    *   *Reference:* [Exportemos Portal](https://exportemos.pe)
*   **[VUCE](https://www.vuce.gob.pe):** (Ventanilla Única de Comercio Exterior). Portal for electronic permits and **Digital Certificates of Origin (COD)** for regional trade.
*   **[MINCETUR](https://www.gob.pe):** The ministry overseeing foreign trade.
*   **[SERPOST / Exporta Fácil](http://www.exportafacil.gob.pe):** The simplified export regime for small businesses and artisans.

## 2. Mining & Legal Frameworks
*   **[INGEMMET](https://www.gob.pe):** (Instituto Geológico, Minero y Metalúrgico). Manages mining concessions and the **National Mining Cadastre**.
*   **[MINEM](https://www.gob.pe):** (Ministerio de Energía y Minas). Oversees **REINFO** (extended through Dec 31, 2026) and the transition to the new **MAPE Law** (Small-Scale and Artisanal Mining).
    *   *Reference:* [REINFO Public Query](https://padron.minem.gob.pe)
*   **[Ley General de Minería](https://www.gob.pe):** (DS Nº 014-92-EM). Primary legislation governing mineral origin.
    *   *Article 4 (Libre Comercialización):* Right to free trade of mineral products.
    *   *Article 6 (Posesión de Minerales):* Legal possession by non-concession holders.
    *   *Article 263 (Comercialización Interna):* Internal commercialization and transformation.

## 3. Cultural & Artistic Heritage
*   **[RNA (Registro Nacional del Artesano)](https://artesania.mincetur.gob.pe):** The official registry for Peruvian artisans managed by **MINCETUR**. Used for metadata verification and artisan recognition.
*   **[Ministerio de Cultura](https://www.gob.pe):** Responsible for certifying that stones are not "Patrimonio Cultural" (National Cultural Heritage).
*   **[SERFOR](https://www.gob.pe):** (Servicio Nacional Forestal y de Fauna Silvestre). Required for specimens involving organic gems or stones from protected forestry zones.

## 4. Technical Infrastructure
*   **[Hedera Developer Docs](https://docs.hedera.com):** Documentation for **HTS** (Token Service) and **HCS** (Consensus Service).
*   **[HIP-412 Metadata Standard](https://hips.hedera.com):** The community standard for NFT metadata on Hedera.
*   **[IPFS (InterPlanetary File System)](https://ipfs.tech):** Decentralized storage for immutable stone imagery and 3D scans.
