# Future Vision

Rumi’s roadmap extends beyond initial compliance automation, aiming to expand utility and reach.

## Phase 1: Boutique Shop
- **Founder‑Operated Store:** Rumi begins as a boutique shop owned and operated by the project founder.  
- **Single Buyer & Seller:** All stones originate from the boutique, ensuring provenance consistency.  
- **Simplified Governance:** Operational keys are held securely by the founder, reducing complexity.  
- **Controlled Compliance:** REINFO, RUC, HS Code, and VUCE checks are performed directly before minting.  
- **Audit Trail:** Customs officers and buyers see a clean chain of custody: boutique → Rumi NFT → export record.

## Phase 2: Expanded Marketplace
- **Multi‑Vendor Participation:** Verified miners, artisans, and exporters onboarded into the Rumi ecosystem.  
- **Distributed Governance:** Transition from founder‑controlled keys to multi‑sig and smart contract governance.  
- **Artisan Marketplace:** Launch a platform where artisans showcase tokenized works with RNA verification.  
- **Regulator Dashboard:** Provide agencies with direct access to compliance proofs and audit trails.  
- **Buyer Experience:** Enhance the Rumi Website with interactive provenance maps, 3D scans, and cultural storytelling.

## Token Evolution
- **Rumi Coin:** Introduce a fungible utility token for transaction fees, artisan rewards, and marketplace incentives.  
- **Smart Contract Transition:** Move supply and compliance logic from private keys to audited smart contracts for transparency and automation.  
- **Cross‑Chain Liquidity:** Enable bridges to Ethereum, Solana, and other ecosystems to expand market access.

## Long‑Term Goals
- **Global Standards Alignment:** Position Rumi as a model for Real World Asset tokenization in emerging markets.  
- **Community Governance:** Transition to a DAO structure where miners, artisans, and buyers vote on protocol upgrades.  
- **Sustainability Integration:** Explore carbon offset tracking and environmental compliance fields in metadata.

---

Rumi’s vision is to evolve from a founder‑operated boutique into a **global provenance network**, balancing cultural heritage protection with modern digital trade.
