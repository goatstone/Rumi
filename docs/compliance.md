# Regulatory & Legal Framework

Rumi tokens operate within the scope of Peruvian mining, labor, and cultural heritage laws. The framework ensures that tokenized assets comply with national regulations and international trade standards.

## Governing Laws
- **Ley General de Minería (DS Nº 014-92-EM):** Establishes the foundation for mineral extraction, commercialization, and export.
- **Ley Nº 32537 (Dec 2025):** Extends REINFO validity to **Dec 31, 2026** and mandates a National Census of Small-Scale Mining (INEI/MINEM) by June 2026.
- **DL 1293 & DL 1107:** Modified by Ley Nº 32537 to require verification of extraction concessions by adquirentes (such as Rumi).
- **SUNAFIL Regulations (2026):** Require REINFO holders to incorporate workers into the formal payroll (Planilla Electrónica) and undergo workplace inspections.

## Authority & Database Mapping
The **Regulatory Handshake** validates assets against official Peruvian government databases before minting:

| Authority | Resource | Validation Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **MINEM** | REINFO Search | Verifies the mining concession is active and the miner is in a state of **Vigente**. |
| **SUNAT** | RUC Consultation | Ensures the shop is a legal commercial entity under **Article 6**. |
| **VUCE** | Permit Verification | Validates export authorizations and Digital Certificates of Origin (COD). |

### 2026 Requirements
- **Proceso de Sinceramiento:** Georeferenced concession coordinates must be submitted via the Ventanilla Virtual. Failure to comply by **April 25, 2026** results in automatic REINFO suspension.
- **Census Gate (Post‑June 2026):** INEI Census Participation ID is required to confirm miner inclusion in the National MAPE Registry.
- **Status Check:** The handshake must confirm **Vigente** status; **Suspendido** now triggers non‑compliance.

## Compliance Obligations
- **REINFO Status:** Assets must be linked to concessions with active REINFO registration.
- **Payroll Verification:** Confirm miners have registered employees under SUNAFIL’s electronic payroll system.
- **Workplace Inspections:** *Res. de Superintendencia Nº 0051-2026-SUNAFIL* approved mandatory workplace inspections of REINFO holders. Rumi’s due diligence includes verifying the existence of the required **Electronic Payroll (Planilla Electrónica)**.
- **Census Participation:** By mid‑2026, miners must provide INEI Census IDs to remain eligible.
- **Cultural Protection:** Stones classified as *Patrimonio Cultural* are blocked from export and tokenization.

## Key Legal Articles
- **Article 4 (Libre Comercialización):** Guarantees free trade of mineral products.
- **Article 6 (Posesión de Minerales):** Defines legal possession rights for retailers and artisans.
- **Article 263 (Comercialización Interna):** Regulates domestic purchase and sale of minerals for transformation or export.

## Enforcement & Oversight
- **Regulatory Handshake:** Automated validation against MINEM, SUNAT, VUCE, and INEI databases prior to minting.
- **Audit Trails:** Immutable logs on Hedera Consensus Service (HCS) provide timestamped compliance proofs for customs and regulators.
- **SUNAFIL Inspections:** Workplace audits ensure miners comply with labor formalization requirements.
