## Legal Disclaimer: RWA Compliance & Mineral Commercialization

This disclaimer is for informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. Users remain responsible for compliance with all applicable laws of the Republic of Peru.

### 1. Right of Free Commercialization
In accordance with **Article 4** of the [Ley General de Minería (DS Nº 014-92-EM)](https://www.gob.pe), the internal and external trade of minerals is free (**Libre Comercialización**). No special mining concession is required to buy or sell finished stone products, provided they are acquired through lawful channels. Rumi NFTs reflect seller‑provided documentation only.

### 2. Legal Possession (Non-Concession Holders)
Per **Article 6** and **Article 263** of the same Law, the possession of minerals by artisans, retailers, and collectors is legally recognized. Provenance records in Rumi NFTs establish a digital baseline but do not replace physical documentation requirements.

### 3. Cultural Heritage Restriction
Rumi does not tokenize or facilitate the export of items classified as **Patrimonio Cultural de la Nación** (National Cultural Heritage).  
- In compliance with [Ministerio de Cultura](https://www.gob.pe/cultura) regulations, no stone identified as a pre-Columbian artifact or protected historical object may be minted.  
- The Hedera “Validation Layer” provides a preliminary check but does not supersede official government certification.  

### 4. Export Responsibility
While Rumi metadata is designed to align with [SUNAT](https://www.sunat.gob.pe) and [VUCE](https://www.vuce.gob.pe) requirements, the physical exporter of record remains responsible for the accuracy of **Exporta Fácil** or **DAM** filings. The NFT serves as a supporting digital "Packing List" and "Certificate of Origin," but is not a substitute for final customs clearance issued by Peruvian authorities. Exporters must retain and present physical documentation.

### 5. No Investment Advice
Rumi tokens represent Real World Assets (RWAs). The value of these stones depends on geological rarity, artisanal quality, and market demand.  
- Rumi Coin and associated NFTs are not offered as regulated financial securities.  
- Rumi NFTs are collectibles tied to physical specimens; they are not securities, derivatives, or regulated financial instruments.
